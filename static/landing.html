Features

Everything you need to stay on top

A complete toolkit for tracking video content — from scanning to notifications to watch history.

Automatic Scanning

Set it and forget it. VideoWatch scans your sites on a schedule you control — hourly, daily, or on demand.

Instant Notifications

Get webhook alerts (Discord, Slack, etc.) the moment a new video is detected. Per-site toggle so you only hear about what matters.

Multi-Platform Support

Monitor YouTube channels, VK Video, and custom websites all from one dashboard. Automatic platform detection.

Watch History

Track what you've watched. Videos are automatically marked as watched when you open them, with visual dimming for viewed content.

Favourites & Archive

Star videos to find them later, archive ones you're done with, or ignore content you never want to see again.

Bulk Actions

Select multiple videos at once to mark them seen, watched, archived, or ignored — perfect for catching up after a long absence.

Mobile Friendly

Clean 2-column grid on small screens, compact controls, and a responsive layout that works on any device.

Smart Filtering

Filter by platform, site, new/unwatched/favourites status. Keyword-based scan filtering skips irrelevant listing pages automatically.

Video Downloads

Download videos directly through the app using yt-dlp integration. Queue multiple downloads and track progress in real time.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No complex setup required. Add a URL and VideoWatch handles the rest.

1

Add a site or channel

Paste any video listing URL — a YouTube channel, VK Video page, or any website with video links. VideoWatch auto-detects the platform.

2

Configure your scan schedule

Choose how often to scan — from every 15 minutes to once a day. Set the number of pages to crawl and whether to receive webhook alerts.

3

Get notified instantly

When new videos appear, VideoWatch sends a webhook notification and adds them to your dashboard, marked as new and ready to watch.

4

Track your watch history

Open any video and it's automatically marked as watched. Filter by unwatched, favourites, or archived content any time.
